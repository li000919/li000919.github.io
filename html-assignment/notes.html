Here is the tags I have learnt and their description:

<a>
Defines a hyperlink, which is used to link from one page to another or to a specific section within the same page.
<h1-h6>
Heading tags that define the headings in the document, with <h1> being the highest (most important) and <h6> the lowest.
<p>
Defines a paragraph of text.
<img>
Used to embed images in an HTML document.
<mark>
Highlights text to indicate its importance or relevance, typically used to draw attention to specific content.
